Biography

I am currently a Visiting Research Scholar in Visual Intelligence Lab at Georgia Tech working with Prof. Devi Parikh and Prof. Dhruv Batra. I graduated with an MS in Computer Science and Statistics from Purdue University where I was advised by Prof. Xiao Wang.

Prior to that, I was an undergrad at IIT Kharagpur where I recieved B.Tech + M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering. I was advised by Prof. Partha Pratim Chakrabarti for my B.Tech thesis and Prof. Bivas Mitra for my M.Tech thesis.

I spent a wonderful summer in 2014 at HKUST working with Prof. Pan Hui and in 2013 working at Qualcomm Inc.

Besides research, I enjoy nature, especially mountains. My adventures in the Himalayas are some of the most memorable ones.
